Plan: Internship Management — DB Schema Design         

 Context

 The internship management feature lets companies create and manage internship programs through a 5-section form: Details, Screening Criteria,
 Selection, During Internship, and Final Submission. No Prisma models for this exist yet — the internship-hiring bounded context directory is in
 place but its domain/infrastructure layers are empty (greenfield).

 This plan covers only the DB schema (Prisma models + migration). Backend DDD layers and frontend are separate follow-on tasks.

 ---
 Normalization Decisions

 Folded directly onto internships (avoids joins on the common "load internship" path):
 - Offer documents: offerLetterTemplatePdfUrl, termsConditionsPdfUrl, releaseMethod
 - Process execution: plant (String — no Plant model exists yet), functionalGroupId
 - Pre-internship: preInternshipContent (rich text, Section 3 concern, not Section 1)
 - During: totalWeeks, midtermFeedbackDate

 Kept separate for InternshipAbout: 8 large rich-text fields would bloat the root row and hurt list-view index scans.

 Kept separate for 1-1 children (EligibilityCheck, InterviewRubric, FinalSubmission): each is a distinct lifecycle phase that may be absent in
 draft state, and they are queried independently.
